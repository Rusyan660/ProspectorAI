How does LeadPilot find leads?▾
LeadPilot uses AI to search across 200M+ company records, LinkedIn data, and web signals to find companies that match your ideal customer profile. You can search by industry, location, size, technology stack, and more.
Is there a free trial?▾
Yes! Every new account gets a 14-day free trial with full access to all Pro features. No credit card required.

Our AI scoring model is trained on millions of sales outcomes and achieves 89% accuracy in predicting which leads will convert. Scores consider website quality, digital presence, growth signals, and business activity.
